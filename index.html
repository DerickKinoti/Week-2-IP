Akan Name Finder

Brief Description of the webapp

This is a webpage that enables you to find out what your "Akan Name" would be if you are/were from Ghana. Akan names are derived based on gender,that is whether male or female and day of the week on which a person is born.Do you wanna find out your Akan name? Fill the form below to find out!.
